This onepager is meant to be a short intro to myself, how I work and what drives me.
Building creative tools is what got me into programming and seeing people create with the things I make gives me joy.

I started out in 3D art, my goal was to create visuals for movies. This was my jumping off point into programming.
I built tools for Blender, first for myself, then for other artists.
This led me to discover engineering and design. I built more tools for artists, sold them and started growning my own communities around them. Serpens currently has a few thousand paying users and a great community around it.

The most rewarding part was seeing people build their own lives around my tool. Enabling them to do what they are passionate about is what drove me to go deeper into this world.
I studied Interaction Design and joined Tesla in 2022, where I've been lucky to work with exceptional people in engineering and design.

Paper is exciting to me because it has the potential to do what I believe a good tool should be:
Tools are a necessary evil, our goals as their builders is to remove the friction of bringing an idea to life. With Paper, the tool can become invisible and even inspire creativity.
